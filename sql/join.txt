join is used to combine rows from two or more tables in RDBMS

1) inner join 
    ..return only the records that have matching values from both the tables

2) left join 
    -- RETURNS ALL THE Records from the left table(customer) and matching rows from the right table(orders)

3) right join
 -- RETURNS ALL THE Records from the right table(ORDER) and matching rows from the left table(CUSTOMER)

4) full outer join 
 --- retuns all the the records when ther is a match in either of the table 

5) cross join ---cartesian product
